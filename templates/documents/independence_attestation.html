Independence Attestation

Provided to: ____________________________________  ·  Engagement: ____________________________________  ·  Date: ______________

Prescription Benefit Solutions attests, for this engagement and as a standing matter:

1We accept no compensation, directly or indirectly, from any pharmacy benefit manager: no consulting fees, no data fees, no rebate-aggregation participation, no implementation allowances.
2We are not owned by, and do not own, any brokerage, carrier, PBM, or vendor whose economics are affected by this review's findings.
3Our compensation for this engagement does not depend on which PBM, carrier, or vendor your plan selects.
4Our complete compensation for this engagement, and its source, is disclosed in our engagement agreement.
5Our complete findings, including anything unfavorable to any incumbent vendor or advisor, are delivered to you without filtering by any third party.

These are the five criteria of The Standards for Independent PBM Review (www.rxbs.org/standards), which we publish and hold ourselves to.
