### Key Performance Patterns

1. **Wednesday POV Text is the swing slot.** Highest ceiling in the entire content mix (41,594 impressions on GLP-1) but also the lowest floor (183 on mental health parity). The hook and topic determine everything. Confrontational > informational.

2. **Tuesday visual posts are the reliable performers.** Messy infographics and carousels consistently deliver mid-to-high range impressions with strong repost and save signals. These are the workhorses.

3. **LinkedIn Newsletter is the audience center of gravity.** 566 subscribers at 45% open rate dwarfs Substack (10 subscribers). The cross-channel bridge from LinkedIn Newsletter to Substack is the primary growth mechanism for Substack.

4. **Substack discovery is emerging.** 15% of Monday article traffic came from open.substack.com in week 12, indicating the Substack ecosystem is starting to surface the content organically.

5. **Broker audience is engaged.** Hylant (national insurance brokerage) appeared in demographics for multiple posts in week 12. Senior seniority consistently 26-34% of audience. The content is reaching decision-makers and their advisors.
