# Paid Ads Bank (Prescription Benefit Solutions)

_Created Jun 23, 2026. The bank of paid-ad concepts, copy, and creative direction, maintained monthly by `/ads-research` (`.claude/commands/ads-research.md`). **Status: PRE-LAUNCH** — no ads running yet; this is the launch-ready bank + the data-ingestion hook for when campaigns go live. Companion: `competitor_ad_teardown.md` (what direct competitors + benefits-advisors run). LinkedIn-first._

**Strategy:** paid ads feed the **existing toolkit lead-magnet funnel** that's already proven (ad → toolkit landing page `rxbs.org/toolkit/<slug>` → 5-email welcome sequence → conversation). The funnel is built and live (Velo form + Zapier sequence); paid ads just add a top-of-funnel inflow to it. The first organic proof of the funnel converting is logged (GWCU broker bulk-download → collaboration; Stand Together → contract review/PBR), so the ladder works; paid amplifies it. **Ad shapes mirror what works organically** (shocking hidden-structure fact, decoder, named-adversary/dollar-comparison) adapted to ad format. Brand rules apply (spell out Prescription Benefit Solutions; no em-dash separators; no fabricated stats; clinical-credibility register).

**Autonomy:** `/ads-research` auto-maintains this bank (concepts, copy, competitor findings). **Every actual spend / launch / budget / audience-go-live decision is PROPOSED to OPEN_ITEMS for Ginny's one-time approval — never auto-spent.**

## Launch-ready ad concepts (LinkedIn)

_Each: objective, audience, offer, two hook/copy variants for A/B, creative direction, status. When live, fill the performance block from the LinkedIn Campaign Manager export._

### AD-1 — Shocking-fact → toolkit (cold, plan-sponsor)
- **Objective:** lead-gen (toolkit download) · **Format:** Sponsored single-image or document ad with a lead-gen form (or click to `rxbs.org/toolkit/pbm-compensation`).
- **Audience:** self-funded employers; titles CFO / VP Finance / HR Director / Benefits Director / Total Rewards; company size 200-10,000+; US. Exclude PBM employees.
- **Offer:** PBM Compensation Audit Worksheet (the "where your PBM actually makes money" tool).
- **Hook A:** "Your PBM passes through 100% of your rebates. The middleman it owns does not have to." **Hook B:** "There are five ways your PBM makes money on your plan. Your contract names one of them clearly."
- **Creative:** clean conceptual infographic (money routing around a gate) or the toolkit preview PNG. Plex Sans; Primary Blue. No fabricated figures.
- **Status:** concept ready · **Performance:** _TBD (not live)._

### AD-2 — Decoder → toolkit (cold, plan-sponsor)
- **Objective:** lead-gen · **Audience:** as AD-1.
- **Offer:** Contract Review Readiness Checklist (or the relevant decoder toolkit).
- **Hook A:** "Five fees on your PBM invoice. Five definitions the PBM wrote." **Hook B:** "You cannot negotiate what you have not read. Most plan sponsors have never read their PBM contract."
- **Creative:** annotated-invoice / marked-up-contract preview. **Status:** concept ready · _TBD._

### AD-3 — Broker / advisor partner-enablement (cold, broker)
- **Objective:** lead-gen / partnership · **Audience:** benefits brokers, consultants, advisory-firm principals; titles Producer / Benefits Consultant / Principal; US. (The GWCU win proves brokers convert via the toolkit set.)
- **Offer:** the full toolkit library / a broker-specific framework.
- **Hook A:** "The brokers who win pharmacy conversations bring the contract questions, not just the renewal quote." **Hook B:** "Arm your plan-sponsor clients with the PBM contract questions their PBM hopes they skip."
- **Creative:** toolkit-library product shot. **Status:** concept ready · _TBD._

### AD-4 — Retargeting → consult (warm)
- **Objective:** conversions / book a call · **Audience:** website + toolkit-page visitors, video viewers, profile engagers (retargeting; requires the LinkedIn Insight Tag / audience setup — flag as a prerequisite).
- **Offer:** a contract review / PBR conversation (the high-value engagement, e.g. team@rxbs.org or a booking link).
- **Hook:** "You pulled the toolkit. The next step is the 90 days before your renewal." **Status:** concept ready · _TBD._

### AD-5 — Proof-stat / category-leader (cold, plan-sponsor) — GATED on the public proof rollout
- **Objective:** lead-gen / authority · **Audience:** as AD-1.
- **Offer:** the toolkit + the implicit "we find money" promise. **Differentiation:** the enterprise firms advertise findings-proof (Aon "$60M located"); PBS counters with its **solo** verified 2025 numbers.
- **Hook:** "$78.7M in contracted savings across 203 self-funded plans last year. The savings were already in the contracts." **Anchor:** 132 benefit reviews / avg $469K, the RFP line at 25%.
- **Guardrails:** headline the **contracted** $78.7M (not the $86.7M identified); 25% is the RFP rate, never "average"; PBS claims these solo (separated from Hylant). **Status: HELD** until the public proof-page rollout (Q3/Sep-Oct surge per CLAUDE.md) — do not run before the metrics are public. · _TBD._

## "What keeps you up at night" ad series (anxiety-led, added Jun 23, 2026)
The strongest ad architecture per PBS's data (name-the-discomfort beats informational 10-100x). Built from `buyer_anxiety_map.md`: **one ad per anxiety row**, each stating the fear as the hook, the PBS answer as the body, and the matching toolkit as the offer, targeted to that row's persona. Examples (drawn from the map):
- CFO · "Pharmacy is up double digits and you can't tell the board why." → PBM Disclosure / GER audit.
- CFO · "'We trusted our PBM' is not a fiduciary defense." → Fiduciary Documentation.
- HR · "You can't get a straight answer out of your PBM. The contract made the answer optional." → PBM Disclosure / What I'd Ask.
- Broker · "A client asks a pharmacy question you can't answer deeply." → Toolkit library.
`/ads-research` expands this series each run as new anxieties surface (incl. from the "Ask Ginny" DM questions). AD-1 through AD-4 above are the lead creatives; the series is the scalable long tail.
