# PBS LinkedIn Paid Ads — Pre-Spend Runway & Green-Light Plan

_Created June 2, 2026. Owner: Ginny / PBS. Status: pre-spend (groundwork phase)._

## Verdict (read this first)

**Do not turn on paid spend yet.** Paid is an *amplifier* of PBS's organic engine, not a replacement for it, and three things make spending today premature:

1. **The toolkit funnel is one day old and unvalidated** (live Jun 2, 2026; ~1 real lead so far). We don't yet know the conversion math that makes paid work: of people who download a toolkit, how many book a consultation, and of those, how many become clients.
2. **Organic is still climbing, not plateaued** (followers 1,374 → 1,903 and newsletter 605 → 930 in ~2 months). The marginal dollar is better spent feeding the free engine than buying around it.
3. **The retargeting pool doesn't exist yet.** The highest-ROI paid play (re-touching warm people who already engaged) needs weeks of accumulated site-visitor + engager data, and a LinkedIn audience can't even be *used* until it hits 300 members.

The smart sequence: **free groundwork now → validate the funnel with organic traffic (4-8 weeks) → turn on paid only when the funnel math is known.** This turns paid from an expensive experiment into a calculated multiplier of a proven path.

## Phase 0 — Free groundwork (do this week, $0 spend)

1. **Install the LinkedIn Insight Tag on rxbs.org.** Wix path: Settings → Marketing Integrations → LinkedIn Insight Tag (paste the Partner ID from Campaign Manager), or Settings → Custom Code → paste the tag site-wide in `<head>`. This starts building the retargeting pool immediately, even with zero spend.
2. **Set up the PBS Company Page + Campaign Manager account.** No campaigns, no spend. Just the container, plus a billing method on file so there's no scramble later.
3. **Create the retargeting (Matched) Audiences now so they accrue for free:**
   - All rxbs.org visitors (last 90/180 days)
   - Toolkit landing-page visitors specifically (highest intent)
   - Company Page / post engagers
   - Video viewers (if any video runs)
   - Newsletter / known-contact list upload (for ABM + lookalikes)
   - *Note: each audience must reach **300 members** before LinkedIn lets you target it. The runway naturally builds this.*
4. **Confirm the creative is ready** (the 3 proven-winner ads are spec'd below). No production needed until green-light, but the concepts are locked.

## Green-light thresholds (turn paid ON only when ALL are true)

1. **Funnel math known:** you can fill in the sentence above with real (not guessed) rates from at least ~20-30 organic downloads.
2. **A defensible max cost-per-download (CPL):** derived from LTV (see worked example below). If LinkedIn's likely CPL ($50-150 B2B) sits comfortably under your affordable CPL, paid is viable.
3. **Retargeting pool ≥ 300** (LinkedIn minimum), ideally a few thousand, so warm campaigns can actually run.
4. **Sales capacity confirmed:** someone can work the leads. Generating more leads than PBS can convert is waste, not growth.

### Worked example of the affordable-CPL math (illustrative)

> _Illustrative example for educational purposes. Actual amounts vary by plan and client._
>
> If 10% of downloads book a consultation, and 20% of consultations become clients, then 1 client requires ~50 downloads. If a client is worth $C in first-year fee, you can afford up to roughly `$C ÷ 50` per download and still break even in year one (and far more given multi-year retention). ## When green-lit: the entry sequence (lowest-risk first)

1. **Retargeting-only test (~$1,000-1,500/mo):** show the **$87M proof ad (Ad 3)** to the warm audience built in Phase 0. Cheapest, warmest, fastest signal.
2. **Add ABM (~+$1,500-2,500/mo):** upload a named list of target self-funded employers; show **Ad 1 (decoder)** + **Ad 2 (toolkit)** only to decision-makers at those accounts. Higher efficiency than broad demographic targeting for a high-trust service.
3. **Scale the winner, kill the rest:** once one ad × audience beats the affordable CPL, pour budget there. Only test broad cold targeting *after* retargeting + ABM are proven.

**Paid lead capture:** use LinkedIn **Lead Gen Forms** for paid campaigns (native tracking, pre-filled, no reliance on tracking the on-site custom form). Pipe those leads into the same Zapier email sequence the toolkit funnel uses. The on-site funnel continues serving organic traffic.

**Kill/scale rule:** review every 2 weeks. Scale anything under the affordable CPL; pause anything 50%+ over it after a fair test (≥ ~1,000 impressions / ~$150 spent per ad).

## KPIs

- **Pre-paid (Phase 1):** funnel conversion rates (download → consultation → client). These are the deliverable of the runway.
- **Paid (post green-light):** cost per download, cost per consultation, consultation → client rate, payback period. **Not impressions or reach** — those are vanity here given the small, high-value buyer set.

---

## The $87M claim — handle with care

It's a real, powerful proof point and on-brand (proprietary, unfakeable). Before it runs in *paid*:
- Have a one-line **methodology** ready ("aggregate realized savings across self-funded clients, 2025").
- Add **"Results vary by plan"** to any creative carrying the figure.
- Run it past whoever handles compliance. Paid ads are screenshot-able, scrutinized, and seen by the PBMs being called out, so the downside of a challenged claim is larger than on organic.
