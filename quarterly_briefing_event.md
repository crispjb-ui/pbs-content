## Format

- **Cadence:** quarterly (Sep, Dec, Mar, Jun). Same title every time; it becomes a franchise.
- **Length:** 45 minutes: 25 content + 15 Q&A + 5 close. Midday Eastern (12:00 PM ET Wednesday recommended; plan-sponsor lunch slot).
- **Platform:** Zoom Webinar (registration page = lead capture with name/email/company/role, same fields as the toolkit form) + a **LinkedIn Event** for promotion that links to the Zoom registration. LinkedIn Live is the reach alternative but captures no registrant data; registration IS the point, so Zoom wins. Camera on Ginny, simple slides from the quarterly report.
- **Free, no gate beyond registration.** This is the top of the consult funnel, not a product.
