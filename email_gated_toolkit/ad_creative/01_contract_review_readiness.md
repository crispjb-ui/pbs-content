## Ad creative — Single Image Ad with Lead Gen Form

### Image (1200 × 627 px)

**Concept: Marked-Up Contract Page Photoreal**

> A photorealistic top-down photo, 1200 x 627 pixels (LinkedIn ad horizontal format). An open printed PBM Services Agreement page rests on a dark wood desk under soft natural daylight. The page is slightly off-white (#F5F0E4) with a faint horizontal section header in bold black serif type at the top: "PHARMACY BENEFIT MANAGEMENT SERVICES AGREEMENT." Below the header, dense legalese paragraphs are visible (rendered as recognizable contract type without specific words being legible).
>
> Eight handwritten red ballpoint annotations are drawn in the margins, each pointing at a different clause area, with margin notes:
> - "Rebate definition narrowed at last renewal"
> - "Channel margin clause — silent"
> - "Performance guarantee — no remediation language"
> - "Network exclusion — 12 pharmacies removed, no notice"
> - "Disclosure obligations — 4 of 5 missing"
> - "Termination clause weaker than market"
> - "Definition variance across versions"
> - "PA workflow ≠ contract"
>
> Top of page, in larger handwritten black marker, double-underlined: "Eight findings. One contract review."
>
> Below, in smaller handwritten red pen: "PBS reviews 100s of PBM contracts a year. Most surface 4-6 of these."
>
> A black ballpoint pen rests at the top-right corner. Style: realistic paper texture, soft natural daylight, slightly imperfect handwriting (Caveat / Permanent Marker / Kalam-style). Colors: off-white contract paper, faint black legalese, red handwritten ballpoint, dark wood desk. No logos, no PBM names, no client names.

## Why this is the #1 priority ad

The Contract Review is PBS's first-touch entry product. The strongest possible lead-magnet is the pre-engagement checklist that walks readers through what a Contract Review actually delivers — they self-qualify on the page (their renewal-cycle dropdown answer alone tells PBS how warm the lead is), and the welcome sequence's Email 5 names the Contract Review explicitly as the natural next step.

This ad is the only one that connects directly to PBS's revenue. The other four ad creatives (mechanic-specific worksheets) are educational lead magnets that nurture toward the same Contract Review CTA, but this one shortcuts the funnel.
