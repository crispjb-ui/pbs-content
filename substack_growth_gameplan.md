# Substack Growth Gameplan — waking the sleeping giant

_Created Jul 16, 2026, from Brett's ask ("what's the serious game plan with Substack? I feel like it's a sleeping giant"). This consolidates the Substack-specific levers scattered across the notes plan, the AEO rules, the entity plan, and the Potter track into one prioritized sequence. Companion docs: `substack_notes_plan_2026_07.md` (the Notes trial), `substack_aeo_rules.md` (per-article), `entity_authority_offsite_plan.md` §2 (Recommendations), `substack_engagement_guide.md`._

## The thesis

Substack is the only surface PBS fully owns: **the relationship** (exportable subscriber emails; LinkedIn's 1,170 newsletter subs belong to LinkedIn), **the crawl** (the AI-citation surface; engines cite it, and Perplexity already does), and **the depth** (a competitor's clinical director read one field note 18 times; nobody does that on a feed). The July 2026 evidence says the flywheel is starting on its own: Rising badge #85 → #73 (paid-growth-RATE leaderboard), first organic-Google acquisition, 4 paid subs including a fund office and a fiduciary advisor, all unprompted.

**Goal: ~500 high-fit subscribers by early 2027** (also the trigger for revisiting Ginny's paid hold), prioritizing plan-sponsor-adjacent quality over raw count. Substack subs stay a monitor metric against the two primary KPIs; the giant's value is compounding authority + lead qualification, not sub-count vanity.

## The levers, in priority order

**1. Recommendations (unpulled; the single biggest lever; Ginny, ~5 min).** Substack's mutual-recommendation mechanic is its highest-yield growth path and PBS has never used it. The Potter ask is drafted (`entity_authority_offsite_plan.md` §2) and the relationship is at peak warmth (her piece just ran on his publication). Then 2-3 aligned pubs (drug-pricing/benefits writers Ginny already engages). Measure: subs attributed to "Recommendations" in the growth-source breakdown. **Status: waiting on Ginny's Sprint-2 sitting.**

**2. The Potter byline engine (running; keep cadence).** Every contributor piece is a permanent acquisition asset in front of his audience; 11 more pieces are drafted. Each byline links to Ginny's author profile → Benefit Blind Spots. The restack + X amplification kit per piece (W28 pattern) is now standard.

**3. The Notes flywheel (relaunched Jul 15; 4-week trial running).** Restack-designed reveal notes + creative visuals + restack-with-note launch teasers + Ginny's 1-restack/day habit. The feed is no longer follower-gated and the app is the #1 new-sub source platform-wide, so this is the discovery engine IF the restack metric moves. **Read-out mid-Aug** (`substack_notes_plan_2026_07.md` §4); if restacks stay ~0, Notes drops to maintenance and effort shifts to levers 1-2-4.

**4. Search + AI citations (compounding on autopilot).** Question-shaped SEO titles, answer-first leads, hero posts as rankers (Contract Language Library), outbound canonical links. Already converting (the Google-sourced sub; Perplexity citing spread-pricing + GER prompts). Maintained by the standing AEO rules + freshness registry; no new weekly effort.

**5. Lead qualification depth (the commercial payoff).** The Practitioner's Note paid layer (paid conversion = high-intent hand-raise, logged like a lead), toolkit funnel links in every article, inbound-email behavior, recipient-level engagement watching (the Jake Goll pattern: 18 opens = signal, friendly or competitive). Substack warms the September briefing registrants, the renewal-campaign audience, and the January Index readership.

**6. Seasonal loading.** The Sep-Oct surge and the mid-Jan Index/Fine Print Awards are the two moments Substack gets net-new attention at scale; the levers above determine how much of that attention converts to subscriptions. The Index publishes to Substack + site simultaneously (rollout calendar in `MASTER_GAMEPLAN.md`).

## What the plan is NOT

No sub-count vanity chasing, no growth-hack cross-promo circles, no paid-acquisition spend, no gating of tools/frameworks/answer-leads (the citation surface stays 100% crawlable), no revenue framing on paid (it is a lead signal until the ~500-sub revisit).

## Scoreboard (check monthly at /log-metrics)

- Subs total + growth-source split (Recommendations / app / web / email referral)
- Restacks/week (trial metric) + app-source subs
- Paid conversions (WHO, logged like leads)
- Rising leaderboard position when surfaced
- AI citations of Substack URLs (via `ai_visibility_tracker.md`)
