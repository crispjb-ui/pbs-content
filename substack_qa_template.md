## Guidelines for Answering

- Answer every question, even if the answer is "it depends" (explain what it depends on)
- Be specific. "Review your contract" is not helpful. "Look for the section titled 'Rebate Guarantee' and check whether it says 'gross' or 'net'" is helpful.
- When a question touches on something you have written about, reference the specific issue: "I covered this in detail in Issue #12: [title]"
- When a question is beyond what you can answer without seeing their specific data, say so honestly and suggest what data they should pull
- Never name specific clients or proprietary information
- Include the disclaimer where illustrative examples are used: "Illustrative example for educational purposes. Actual amounts vary by plan."
