# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** [DATE — set when published]
**Last Updated:** [DATE — set when published]

Prescription Benefit Solutions LLC ("PBS," "we," "our," "us") respects your
privacy. This Privacy Policy describes the information we collect, how we
use it, and your choices regarding that information when you visit
rxbs.org, subscribe to our publications, download our Plan Sponsor Toolkit
materials, or otherwise engage with PBS digitally.

## What information we collect

When you submit a form on our website, subscribe to our newsletters, or
respond to a LinkedIn lead generation ad, we collect:

- **Name** (first and last)
- **Email address**
- **Company name and job title**
- **Optional context** you provide in custom fields (e.g., role
  classification, renewal-cycle stage)

When you visit rxbs.org, we collect standard web analytics:

- **IP address and approximate geographic location**
- **Browser type and operating system**
- **Pages visited, time on page, referral source**
- **Cookies as outlined below**

We do not collect protected health information (PHI), claims data,
member-level information, or any information regulated under HIPAA through
this website. Information of that nature is handled exclusively through
secure, contractually governed channels separate from this site.

## How we use the information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Deliver the materials you requested** (Plan Sponsor Toolkit PDFs,
   newsletter subscriptions)
2. **Send you a welcome email sequence** related to the Toolkit you
   downloaded, plus occasional updates about new PBS content or services
3. **Improve our website and content** by analyzing usage patterns
4. **Respond to inquiries** you submit through contact forms
5. **Comply with legal obligations** including marketing consent records

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for their
marketing purposes.

## Cookies and analytics

rxbs.org uses cookies for:

- **Essential functionality** (form submissions, language preferences)
- **Analytics** through Wix Analytics
- **Marketing attribution** (UTM parameters from email and ad campaigns, and
  the LinkedIn Insight Tag, a pixel that supports ad measurement and
  audience building for LinkedIn campaigns)
- **Repeat-download convenience** — when you download a Plan Sponsor Toolkit,
  we store your name, email, company, and role locally in your browser so you
  do not have to re-enter them the next time you request another toolkit. This
  information stays on your device, is used only to pre-fill the download form,
  and can be cleared at any time through your browser settings or the "Not you?"
  option on the form.

You can disable cookies and local storage through your browser settings.
Doing so may affect some site functionality, including the repeat-download
convenience above.

## Third-party services we use

- **Wix** — hosts rxbs.org and the email-marketing infrastructure. Wix
  privacy policy: https://www.wix.com/about/privacy
- **LinkedIn** — the LinkedIn Insight Tag runs on rxbs.org for ad
  measurement and audience building, and if you submit a Lead Gen Form on
  LinkedIn, your information is shared between LinkedIn and PBS per
  LinkedIn's terms. LinkedIn privacy policy:
  https://www.linkedin.com/legal/privacy-policy
- **Substack** — when you subscribe to Benefit Blind Spots on Substack,
  your information is shared between Substack and PBS per Substack's
  terms. Substack privacy policy: https://substack.com/privacy
- **Zapier** — bridges website form submissions to our email delivery
  system so we can send the materials you requested. Zapier privacy
  policy: https://zapier.com/privacy
- **Microsoft** — our toolkit-delivery and follow-up emails are sent
  through Microsoft Outlook. Microsoft privacy statement:
  https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement

## Data retention

We retain your contact information as long as you remain subscribed to our
emails or until you request deletion. Web analytics data is retained per
Wix Analytics' standard retention period.

If you unsubscribe from our emails, we retain your email address only to
ensure we honor your unsubscribe preference and do not re-add you to our
lists.

## Your choices and rights

You may at any time:

1. **Unsubscribe** from PBS emails using the unsubscribe link in any email
   we send
2. **Request access** to the information we have about you
3. **Request correction** of inaccurate information
4. **Request deletion** of your information (subject to record-keeping
   requirements)
5. **Opt out of marketing communications** while still receiving
   transactional emails (e.g., download confirmations)

To exercise any of these rights, email team@rxbs.org with your request.

## California privacy rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the
California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know what
information we collect, the right to delete that information, and the
right to opt out of sale (we do not sell information). To exercise these
rights, email team@rxbs.org.

## EU privacy rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights
under the General Data Protection Regulation, including access,
rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and
the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. To exercise
these rights, email team@rxbs.org.

PBS is a small US-based consulting firm. We use third-party services
(Wix, LinkedIn, Substack) that may transfer EEA residents' data to the
US under Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent data-transfer
mechanisms.

## Security

We use commercially reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to
protect your information, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS for the
website) and access controls on internal systems. No transmission over
the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children

rxbs.org is intended for business audiences. We do not knowingly collect
information from children under 16.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the
"Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be
communicated via email to active subscribers.
