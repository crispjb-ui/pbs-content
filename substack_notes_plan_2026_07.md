# Substack Notes Plan — the restack reset (July 2026)

Status: **PROPOSED 4-week trial** (W29-W32). Rule-of-record change to the CLAUDE.md Notes Strategy table happens only after the trial reads out (approve → encode + `/propagate-rule` per the standing loop)._

## 1. The diagnosis (why it feels like a void)

- The current Notes mix (14 origin/personal + 3 teasers + 4 rotating per week) was locked in **April 2026** on data collected under the OLD feed, which distributed notes mostly to followers. PBS has ~120 Substack subscribers, so 21 notes/week into a ~120-person pond correctly feels like typing into a void.
- **Verified July 2026 (platform_playbooks.md):** the Notes feed is **no longer follower-gated**. Most impressions come from readers who never followed you; distribution is algorithm-amplified; **the restack is the amplification unit** (one restack = the note enters that person's followers' feeds); the Substack app is now the **#1 source of new subscribers, above Recommendations**.
- Consequence: the April finding ("origin stories are the only format that gets engagement") measured *follower warmth*, not *feed reach*. A stranger-fed algorithm rewards a different thing: the self-contained, quotable, restack-worthy claim. That is PBS's home turf — it is the shocking-structural-reveal shape in 280-ish words instead of a LinkedIn image.
- Also verified: **~1 in 3 notes now carry a visual** and Substack redesigned the media tab into a scrollable feed. Text-only-every-time leaves reach on the table. PBS has a library of on-brand visuals (toolkit preview PNGs, Tue/Thu infographic heroes, Library slides) that no competitor can match.

## 2. What changes (and what doesn't)

**Unchanged:** 3 notes/day cadence (brand-fit ceiling, holds against the vendor "5-7/day" churn advice) · launch-day teasers (Mon/Wed/Thu) · Ginny's daily engagement with other writers · all voice/brand rules (no em dashes, proprietary anchors, Humanize Check, reveal-reservation: a week's reveal material never runs on Notes before its LinkedIn post publishes).

**Changed: the slot design.** Every note is written to one test: **would a stranger who has never heard of Prescription Benefit Solutions restack this?**

| Slot | Old (April 2026 mix) | New (trial mix) |
|---|---|---|
| Morning (7-8 AM) | Origin/personal (or launch teaser) | **Reveal note** (or launch teaser on Mon/Wed/Thu): one hidden-mechanism fact, self-contained, quotable, with the PBS anchor. Source: `shocking_fact_bank.md` + the week's published posts. |
| Midday (12-1 PM) | Origin/personal or rotating | **Visual note**: a toolkit preview PNG / Tue-Thu hero / Library slide + 2-3 lines that stand alone (not "new post out" — say the thing the visual proves). |
| Afternoon (4-5 PM) | Origin/personal | **Origin/personal** (kept: the proven warmth format, and the format that converts a curious stranger into a subscriber once a reveal note pulled them in). |

Weekly shape: ~7 reveal-style (3 replaced by launch teasers), ~7 visual, ~7 origin/personal. The reveal and visual slots draw from content that is already built every week, so drafting load in PART 4 stays flat.

**The restack-worthiness test for the reveal slot (the Notes version of the 7-element formula):**
1. First line is the whole claim ("Your PBM passes through 100% of rebates. The rebate aggregator does not have to.")
2. Self-contained: a reader with zero context loses nothing.
3. Named actor + named victim; a number where verified.
4. One PBS anchor (hundreds of contracts a year; the specific clause; the scene).
5. No link in the note body for the reveal slot (a restack is the goal, not a click; launch teasers keep their links).

## 3. New behaviors (the active layer, ~10 min/day, mostly Ginny)

1. **Restack-with-note 1/day:** restack one strong note or article from the lane (Potter, drug-pricing writers, benefits people) adding ONE net-new line per the comment-value rules. Reciprocity is how small accounts get their first restacks back, and it puts Benefit Blind Spots into those threads.
2. **Reply to every comment on PBS notes within the day** (multi-party threads amplify on Substack same as LinkedIn).
3. **Restack her own Monday/Thursday articles once each** with a one-line note that adds a fact (not "ICYMI").

## 4. Measurement (what "not a void" looks like)

Log weekly in `linkedin_performance_tracker.md` (Substack section) via `/log-metrics`:
- **Restacks per week** (primary; baseline ≈ 0)
- Note followers + subscribers with source = Substack app (the funnel the research says is now #1)
- Comments from non-subscribers (stranger reach proxy)

**Read-out after 4 weeks (mid-Aug):** if reveal/visual notes are earning restacks and app-source subs while origin notes hold warmth, lock the mix into CLAUDE.md's Notes Strategy table + `/propagate-rule` the PART 4 drafting change. If restacks stay ≈ 0 across 4 weeks, the void is structural (audience not on Notes), and Notes drops to maintenance cadence so effort moves to channels that pay.
