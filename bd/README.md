## Standard partner-outreach sign-off + firm facts (from Ginny's worked Cristy email, Jun 10, 2026)

Use these verbatim in partner-outreach drafts so they read in Ginny's voice and carry the unfakeable specifics:

- **Signature block:**
  ```
  - Ginny

  Ginny Crisp, PharmD, BCACP | Chief Executive Officer
  O (843) 867.3401
  M (919) 448.1742
  ginny@rxbs.org
  www.rxbs.org
  ```
  (Partner outreach signs from **ginny@rxbs.org** with her direct lines, not team@rxbs.org.)
- **Team specificity (proprietary anchor):** "PBS (3 pharmacists, 3 analysts, and admin support)."
- **Capabilities line:** "we review hundreds of PBM contracts a year and run independent claims and contract/performance audits on the pharmacy side. We also facilitate the RFP process if clients need to take their business to market."
- **Partner framing (not a no-cost-bench pitch):** "we would be happy to partner with your organization to help your clients maximize their PBM contract. The broker stays the relationship owner the whole way."
- **Scheduling:** offer a meeting via **Traci** ("if you'd like to meet, Traci would be more than happy to help set up some time"), not a bare mailto.
- **Personal anchor when one exists:** Ginny is a UNC/Chapel Hill Tarheel — use a genuine personal connection only when real (e.g., a fellow Tarheel for an NC contact); never manufacture one.
