What We Do

One narrow thing, done deeply.

We are pharmacists who audit PBMs for self-funded employers. Not a broker, not a coalition, not a rebate aggregator. An independent set of eyes on the contract, the claims, and the money that flows between them.

01

PBM Contract Audit

We read the PBM Services Agreement line by line, decode the definitions, and surface the clauses quietly costing the plan, with the redlines to fix them.

02

Claims Review

Twelve months of pharmacy claims, pressure-tested against what we see in the market. A dollar figure you can take to renewal, not a narrative.

03

Contract Language Development

Protective pricing, rebate, audit-rights, and termination language drafted from hundreds of contracts a year, so the next agreement defends the plan.

04

Pharmacy Benefit Review

A twice-yearly, six-category audit that keeps oversight continuous and turns findings into your renewal agenda.

05

RFP & Go-to-Market

When the answer is to leave, we score the RFP on what actually matters and backplan the transition so members never feel the switch.

06

Behind Your Broker

We work behind brokers and consultants, never around them. The independent audit bench you bring to your clients; you stay the relationship owner.
