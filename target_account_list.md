# Target Account List — the Named-Account Layer

**Created:** July 3, 2026 (world-class-gap follow-up to the 6-month strategy audit). **Candidate pool researched and added same day** (three parallel research passes; method notes below). Worked inside the Friday sales hour (`email_gated_toolkit/sales_hour_runbook.md`, step 3b); feeds the two primary KPIs directly.

**What this is NOT:** a cold-outreach list. PBS does not cold-pitch. The motion is orchestrated warmth: show up where these accounts already are (their people's posts, their downloads, their profile views), put the right asset in front of them, and let the funnel's own mechanics (toolkits, briefings, PARTNER alerts) convert.

**Status ladder:** `CANDIDATE` (researched fit, unconfirmed) → `COLD` (Ginny-confirmed target, no interaction) → `AWARE` (they've engaged with content) → `WARM` (2+ engagements or a download) → `IN CONVERSATION` (handed to the lead Sheet) → `CLIENT/PARTNER`.

**Ginny's pass (30-60 min):** scan the candidate tables; strike anything that conflicts with a relationship (a Hylant client, a broker's existing account, a known bad fit) — struck rows move to the exclusions section, never silently deleted; promote keepers to COLD; add anyone the research missed. ★ = research-prioritized.

## 2. How accounts get onto the list (sourcing passes, run monthly)

1. **Downloaders:** every toolkit lead's company (the Sheet); criteria match → row.
2. **Engagers:** commenters/repeat reactors on PBS posts; profile-viewer harvest after breakouts.
3. **Audience segments:** recurring company names in LinkedIn post analytics (10,001+ and Hospitals & Health Care segments).
4. **Event registrants:** quarterly briefing list (`quarterly_briefing_event.md`) once live.
5. **Ginny's market knowledge + this research pool** (below).

## 3. The weekly motion (5-10 min inside the sales hour)

Pick **3 accounts** (WARM first, then AWARE, then COLD; CANDIDATEs only after Ginny confirms them). One deliberate value-touch each: substantive comment on their person's post, the ONE matching toolkit with a 2-line note, or a briefing invite. Broker rows execute through `broker_partner_track.md`. Log the touch. Two engagements back or a download = promote the row.

## 6. Guardrails

- Never let the list turn touches into pitches; the touch is value or nothing.
- Broker rows follow the broker-as-hero rules in `broker_partner_track.md` without exception; a candidate broker is never approached about a specific employer also on this list (their client relationships come first).
- CANDIDATE rows are unconfirmed research: **verify the flagged items (PEBA status, pharmacy-line depth, post-acquisition independence) before any deliberate touch.**
- No scraping or purchased lists; person-level info stays at "title to find" (CFO, VP Benefits, EB practice leader) until a real interaction exists.
- Review quarterly: prune 2-quarters-cold rows; the worked list stays ~50 employers / ~20 brokers, not 500.
