# Repurpose Queue: Viral Hits
## Making Sure Top-Performing Posts Earn Multiple Impressions

**Purpose:** Three LinkedIn posts have generated 80%+ of all-time reach. The audience has 3x'd since the first one dropped. Re-distributing these as fresh-looking Substack essays, Notes quote teasers, and periodic re-shares turns one-hit reach into compounding reach.

**Governing principle:** Do not just re-post the same copy. Add a "what has changed since" angle each time the post is repurposed. That gives the original a current hook and positions Ginny as someone who tracks her own claims over time.

---

## Asset 1: GLP-1 Reality Check

**Original:** Feb 11, 2026. Text post. **41,594 impressions** (all-time #1).

**Why it worked:** Specific clinical framing on a topic everyone was discussing but few were discussing accurately. Ginny's credibility as a clinical pharmacist made the reality check land differently than a broker or consultant saying the same thing.

### Repurpose schedule

| Channel | Form | Timing | Hook angle |
|---------|------|--------|------------|
| Substack deep dive | Full essay, 1,400 words | Week 18 (Drug Pipeline) Monday can pull GLP-1 forward as the anchor | "What I said about GLP-1 in February, and what has changed" |
| Substack Note | Pull quote + "revisiting this" frame | 3 months post-original (May 2026) | "Three months after I posted this, the coverage picture has changed" |
| LinkedIn repost with new commentary | Quote-share your own post | 6 months post-original (August 2026) | "Reposting this because every point still stands" |
| Field note follow-up | Thursday tactical | When an FDA action or major payer decision creates news hook | "The GLP-1 coverage decision framework I use now" |

### Content hooks to write when repurposing

- Oral GLP-1 approval (orforglipron approved April 2026) changes the eligible population
- Biosimilar timeline compression
- Employer cost exposure at expanded indications (NASH, sleep apnea, kidney disease)
- Stop-loss attachment point stress at current price points
