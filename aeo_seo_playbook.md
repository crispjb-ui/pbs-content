## 1. Answer-engine citation behavior (ChatGPT search, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, Claude)
- **Answer-first, self-contained passages get cited.** Engines lift a quotable sentence that answers the query without surrounding context. PBS rule already enforces this (open with one quotable answer sentence). **CONFIRMED** (multiple AEO sources, 2025-2026).
- **Per-engine source bias (optimize per engine, don't assume one win carries):** ChatGPT favors authoritative long-form + is the only engine that meaningfully cites LinkedIn (~4% of its citations); Perplexity favors fresh, well-cited articles and leans hardest on Reddit/community + comparison content; Google AIO favors passage quality over rank (see §6). **CONFIRMED/DIRECTIONAL** (Profound + multiple 2026 citation studies).
- **Comparison-listicle / "best X for Y" / how-to formats out-cite standard blog posts.** Listicles lead AI citations (~25-32% share of cited content); comparison + how-to + Q&A structures are pulled disproportionately. For PBS's consideration queries (best/independent PBM audit firm, PBM audit vs broker review) the winning owned format is a genuine comparison/guide page, not a narrative post. **CONFIRMED** (2026 GEO studies; aligns with PBS's `/compare-pbm-audit-vs-broker-review` + `/guides/how-to-choose-a-pbm-auditor` build). _(Measured-data tie-in: `ai_visibility_tracker.md` shows PBS ⬜ on every consideration query, lost to Reddit/listicles/Innovative Rx — this is the format that closes it.)_
- **Entity clarity matters.** Spell out "Prescription Benefit Solutions" + consistent `sameAs` across the web so engines disambiguate the entity (the bare "PBS" collides with Public Broadcasting Service). **CONFIRMED** (matches CLAUDE.md naming rule rationale; validated by the Jul 15 tracker flip — the Ginny-entity prompt went cited on all 3 engines the same week the About/Wikidata/Bing-Places entity stack corroborated one identity).
- **Freshness + structure** help selection; clean headings, lists, and a direct Q→A block are pulled more than dense narrative. **DIRECTIONAL.**
- _(Refresh monthly: any shift in how each engine selects/cites; new citation surfaces.)_

## 2. Structured data / schema
- **Load-bearing types for PBS:** `Organization` + `Person` (with `sameAs`), `Article`/`BlogPosting`, `FAQPage`, `DefinedTermSet`/`DefinedTerm` (glossary), `HowTo` (guides), `WebSite` + `SearchAction`, `BreadcrumbList`, `VideoObject`. Already built into the site blueprints (31 JSON-LD blocks). **CONFIRMED** (schema.org + Google Search Central).
- **FAQ + HowTo rich results are DEAD as SERP features — but keep the schema.** Google **retired the FAQ rich result May 7, 2026** (deprecation notice May 8; feature docs removed Jun 15, 2026; Search Console filter support ended Jun 2026, API ends Aug 2026). `HowTo` rich results were gone since 2023. **Neither schema type is deprecated at schema.org, and Google confirmed it still PARSES FAQPage markup to understand a page** — and the Q&A / step structure is still one of the easiest patterns for AI engines (AIO, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini) to extract and cite. **So: keep FAQPage/HowTo markup, but expect ZERO Google-SERP rich-result lift from it; its value is now AI extraction + entity understanding, carried by the *visible* Q&A/step structure, not the markup.** Do not build new pages *for* the rich result; build them for the liftable answer. **CONFIRMED** (Google Search Central updates log, May 8 / Jun 15 2026; corroborated across 6+ SEO sources). _(PBS note: this reframes, not removes, the queued FAQ hub + the `HowTo` on the 3 guides — see the OPEN_ITEMS proposal.)_
- **Markup must match visible content** (fake/mismatched schema risks a manual action). **CONFIRMED.**
- **Multimodal + schema correlates with higher AI-Overview selection.** Third-party studies report text+image+video+structured-data pages selected at markedly higher rates (~+156%, up to ~+317% with full multimodal+schema; ~+73% for merely structured pages). Google has NOT confirmed a schema ranking signal for AIO, so treat as observational: on answer pages, pair the liftable text with a real image and (where natural) an embedded video, plus `Article`/`VideoObject` schema. **DIRECTIONAL** (SEOcrawl / digitalapplied / wellows analyses, 2026).
- **schema.org v30.0 (Mar 19, 2026)** added `Credential`, `Error`, `floorLevel`, `jobDuration` — vertical-specific, none load-bearing for PBS. **CONFIRMED** (schema.org release notes).
- _(Refresh monthly: new/newly-weighted types, rich-result + AI-Overview eligibility changes, deprecations.)_

## 6. Engine-specific retrieval facts (added 2026-07-03 plan review)
- **ChatGPT retrieves via Bing's index + OAI-SearchBot.** Bing Webmaster Tools verification + IndexNow (Wix-native) are prerequisites for ChatGPT visibility. **CONFIRMED** (OpenAI bot docs + multiple 2026 analyses).
- **Claude search runs on Brave's index** (~87% citation overlap with Brave organic). Clean, fast, server-rendered HTML wins there. **CONFIRMED** (Anthropic subprocessor list; RivalHound study).
- **Google AI Overviews/AI Mode decoupled from rank (deepened in 2026):** top-10 organic supplied ~76% of AIO citations mid-2025 → ~38% early 2026 (Ahrefs) → now reported in the **~17-54%** range early-2026 across studies. Retrieval is passage-level via query **fan-out** (~9-11 sub-queries per prompt), and **pages that rank for the fan-out sub-queries are ~161% more likely to be cited than pages ranking only for the visible query** — so every H2/H3 must stand alone as a complete answer to a *sub-question*, not just the headline query. AIO now appears in ~55% of Google searches. **CONFIRMED** (Ahrefs; SEOcrawl / wellows / quickseo 2026).
- **Engines barely overlap** (~11% of domains cited by both ChatGPT and Perplexity) — optimize per-engine, don't assume one win carries. **CONFIRMED** (Profound citation study).
- **Branded web mentions are the strongest single AI-visibility predictor** (Ahrefs 75K-brand study: 0.664 correlation vs 0.218 for backlinks — ~3x). **~82% of AI-cited links come from earned media** (Muck Rack, 1M+ cited links) and **unlinked brand mentions initiate ~60% of new Knowledge Panels** vs ~35% for backlinks. Digital PR / listicle inclusion is a first-order GEO tactic. **CONFIRMED.**
- **YouTube is the single strongest individual mention signal** (Ahrefs: ~0.737 correlation with AI visibility, above overall branded mentions). Video titles, descriptions, and transcripts that name **Prescription Benefit Solutions** + **Ginny Crisp, PharmD** feed the entity graph the engines read. PBS already produces 9:16 clips + has long-form podcast appearances but has no owned YouTube presence — a live gap (see OPEN_ITEMS proposal). **CONFIRMED** (Ahrefs 75K-brand study, 2026).
- **Reddit + Quora own the consideration/comparison stage.** Combined they match or exceed vendor-owned citations (Reddit+Quora ≈ 21.6% of ChatGPT citations, ≈ 31.4% of Perplexity; vendor-owned only ~28% of ChatGPT). "Best X for Y" / "X vs Y" queries surface community threads *before* vendor sites. This is exactly PBS's ⬜ zone (consideration + contract-language prompts). Winning it = genuine credentialed participation + owned comparison pages, NOT planted/astroturfed answers (brand-risk; see OPEN_ITEMS). **CONFIRMED** (2026 B2B GEO citation studies).
- **Publish on the owned domain FIRST, then syndicate; keep the citation surface crawlable.** 2026 guidance is explicit that domain-first-then-syndicate beats Substack/LinkedIn/Medium-only, and **paywalled Substack underperforms its subscriber count in citation weight** ("if the engines can't crawl the words, the newsletter doesn't exist to them"). This directly **validates PBS's standing strategy** — free Substack as the crawlable citation surface, rxbs.org canonical twins for evergreen, paid-Substack ON HOLD (CLAUDE.md Jul 3). Measured-data-aligned: keep the citation-bearing content free + build the owned twins. **CONFIRMED** (multiple 2026 AEO analyses).
- **Ghost citations:** ~62% of AI citations never name the brand (Semrush, 378K citations). Counter-tactic: put the firm name in the same sentence as every quotable proprietary stat. **CONFIRMED.**
- **Freshness premium:** AI-cited URLs ~26% fresher than organic top-10; ~half of cited content <13 weeks old. Substantive quarterly refreshes with visible + schema `dateModified`; date-bumping without changes is detected and penalized. **CONFIRMED/DIRECTIONAL.**
- **llms.txt: Google formally stated (Jun 2026) it ignores it**; OpenAI log data shows near-zero fetches; Wix now auto-generates one. Keep at zero further investment. **CONFIRMED.**
- **New measurement surfaces (2026):** Google Search Console generative-AI performance report (Jun 2026 rollout; keep the AI-grounding toggle ON), GA4 native "AI Assistant" channel (May 2026), **Wix AI Visibility Overview** (native citation/mention/sentiment tracking in Wix Analytics — enable it). 35-70% of AI referrals arrive referrer-less; the form source field stays ground truth. **CONFIRMED.**
- **Agentic browsers** (ChatGPT Atlas, Perplexity Comet) fill forms — keep lead forms simple labeled fields, no CAPTCHA walls. **DIRECTIONAL.**
