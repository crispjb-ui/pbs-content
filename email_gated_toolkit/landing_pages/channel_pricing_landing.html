What is in the worksheet

A complete audit framework you can run in half a day.

PASS 01

Pull the channel-pricing data

Open your most recent quarterly report. Pull per-channel net cost across retail, mail, and specialty. The data is in your claims; the framing is in the worksheet.

PASS 02

Calculate the spread

Run the 5-line calculation on per-channel net cost. The pattern PBS sees most often: PBM-owned specialty channel runs materially higher than the network-membership clause suggests.

PASS 03

Compare the contract clause

Open the channel-pricing clause in your contract. Compare the contractual language to the actual routing pattern. The gap is your renewal-leverage item.

ACTION PLAN

4-step quarter plan

Pull the data. Calculate the spread. Compare to the contract. Bring the documented gap to your broker before the next quarterly review. The worksheet has the data-request copy you can paste directly into the email.

Who uses this

Plan sponsors, finance teams, and brokers running independent PBM oversight.

The worksheet is built for self-funded employers who want to see what their PBM contract actually permits versus what their plan is actually paying. Hand it to your PBM account team or your broker as the agenda for the next quarterly review.
