# Weekly Sales Hour — Runbook

**Created:** July 3, 2026 (approved from the 6-month strategy audit). One protected hour per week. This is the highest-converting hour on Ginny's calendar; content fills the funnel, this hour empties it. Suggested slot: Friday morning after the 8:30 tease post ships, but any consistently-kept hour works.

**Where:** the "PBS Toolkit Leads" Google Sheet (Leads tab) + team@rxbs.org inbox + `broker_partner_track.md` tracker.

---

## The hour, in order (stop when the hour ends; the order guarantees the highest-value work happens first)

**1. New hot items first (~15 min).** Every SQL / PARTNER alert and call request from the past 7 days that has no `last_action` yet:
- SQL buyers → personal note (a two-line "saw you grabbed X, happy to point you at what to check first" reads better than any template).
- PARTNER brokers → Template P1 from `broker_partner_track.md`.
- Repeat downloaders (3+, or SQL/PARTNER on 2nd) → Email 8 template.
- Update `status`, `last_action`, `owner` on each row as you go. Goal: no hot lead older than 7 days without a personal touch.

**2. Replies and open threads (~15 min).** Anything in the inbox from a lead, a briefing reply, or a scheduling thread that has stalled: answer it or nudge it. A warm thread that goes quiet for two weeks is a dead thread.

**3. One broker move (~10 min).** Open `broker_partner_track.md`, take the top row with a defined next step, and do the next step (send the kit, propose the three-way call, quarterly check-in). One move per week compounds to the 3-5 relationship target by December.

**3b. Three account touches (~5-10 min, added Jul 3, 2026).** Open `target_account_list.md`, pick 3 accounts (WARM first), and do ONE deliberate value-touch each per that file's motion (substantive comment, matched toolkit with a 2-line note, briefing invite). Log the touch in the row. This is the named-account layer; without it the list is decoration.

**4. Stale-promising sweep (~10 min).** Filter the Sheet for MQL+ rows with `last_action` older than ~30 days. Pick the two most promising; send each a one-line, zero-pressure note tied to something current ("the renewal-window piece this week made me think of your situation").

**5. Log and close (~5 min).** Every touch recorded in the Sheet (that is what makes next week's hour fast). Note the week's counts: new leads, touches made, calls booked, calls held.

---

## Rules

- **The hour is protected.** It moves, it never cancels. Skipping it silently converts the whole content system back into a reach hobby.
- **Personal beats polished.** Two specific sentences from Ginny outperform any sequence email. Templates are floors, not scripts.
- **Never more than one unanswered touch per lead per month** (except the scheduled 06/07 emails, which don't count as personal touches).
- **Scheduling stays the admin's:** any "yes" gets the admin cc'd immediately; Ginny never plays calendar tennis.
- **Monthly rollup (last hour of the month, 10 extra minutes):** count qualified conversations held and broker rows advanced; that is the scoreboard for the two primary KPIs (4-6 conversations/month by October; 3-5 broker relationships by year end).
