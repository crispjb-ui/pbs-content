Tier 1 · PBM Contract Review · Readiness Checklist

Pull Eight Documents. Surface Fifteen Audits.

The readiness checklist Prescription Benefit Solutions uses across hundreds of PBM contract reviews a year. A 2-page printable framework, free, sent to your inbox.

  • 8-document set to pull before the review kickoff
  • 15 per-mechanic audit components mapped (financial, contract, workflow)
  • Optimize-existing vs go-to-market recommendation with dollar magnitudes
  • Paste-ready broker email to request what's missing

Free. No spam. Educational; results vary by plan.

What's inside

Four moves the checklist walks you through.

Documents to Gather

Pull eight items before kickoff: current PBM Services Agreement and amendments, prior versions, last four quarterly reports, Plan Document, stop-loss policy, prior RFP docs, member-disruption logs. Whatever's missing is a finding by itself.

Fifteen Audits Mapped

Every review integrates fifteen per-mechanic audits across financial, contract-clause, and workflow categories. Each runs standalone in half a day.

The Decision It Produces

Every review ends with one of two calls: optimize existing (negotiate redlines) or go to market (issue an RFP). The framework walks the three variables behind it.

This Quarter's Action Plan

Pull the set, schedule a 60-90 minute kickoff, convene the benefits committee to receive findings, then continue under a twice-yearly Pharmacy Benefit Review cadence.
