## §1 · Monday original — Find your pharmacist (B1, Both Sides of the Counter)

```
An underused resource for any CFO trying to understand pharmacy benefits: your own health system's pharmacy director. And if you are not in healthcare, the pharmacy leaders in your network.

These are people who live with drug pricing every day. They know what a medication actually costs to acquire, what the aggregators do, what happens between the list price and the counter. They have sat across from the same vendors your plan negotiates with, wearing a different hat.

For years I was one of them, and here is the thing: almost nobody from the benefits side ever asked us anything.

The finance office and the pharmacy department of the same institution can go years without comparing notes on the drug supply chain they are both paying into.

If you own a benefits budget, find your pharmacist. Buy them coffee. Ask what surprised them most about drug pricing. I promise the hour pays for itself.
```
