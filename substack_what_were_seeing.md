1. **Only PBS can produce** (proprietary insight from contract review volume)

## Writing Guidelines

- **Use qualifiers, not percentages.** "A majority of contracts" not "73% of contracts." Specific percentages imply a formal study; qualifiers reflect pattern observation.
- **Be specific about what to check.** "Review your rebate guarantee section" is useful. "Review your contract" is not.
- **The negotiation ask section is the most valuable.** This is what brokers and consultants will share with their clients. Make it actionable.
- **Keep it short.** 500-700 words total. Density over length. Every sentence should contain either an observation or an action.
- **No client identification.** Not even anonymized case studies that could be reverse-engineered by someone who knows your client list.
