## Performance-Based Format Strategy (Updated Feb 2026)

### Key Learnings From Q1 Post Analytics

**TOP PERFORMERS (10K+ impressions):**
1. "1 in 12" GLP-1 number - TEXT (64K) - Tease hook format
2. "Generic drugs cost pennies" - INFO (38K) - Exposé hook
3. Express Scripts news - LINK (26K) - Industry controversy
4. Copay Accumulators - CAR (19K at 3d) - Hidden program exposé
5. Rebate Confusion Guide - CAR (18K) - "We pass through all rebates" hook

**DEAD FORMAT:**
- Native Polls: 3 polls averaging 300 impressions, 0 engagement
- RECOMMENDATION: Replace ALL polls with Tease Hook text posts

### What Works - The "Exposé" Pattern

Every top performer exposes something hidden:
- Hidden stat ("1 in 12" - the number nobody talks about)
- Hidden cost ("pennies vs dollars")
- Hidden practice ("flipped drugs", "copay accumulators")
- Hidden lie ("we pass through all rebates")

### Hook Templates That Drive Virality

1. "Here's the [topic] number nobody's talking about. [Number]."
2. "[Thing] costs [small amount]. So why does your plan pay [big amount]?"
3. "Your PBM says [claim]. Here's what that actually means."
4. "[Number]% - That's [stat that creates outrage/curiosity]"
5. "Here's the contract phrase that costs employers the most. Two words."

### Visual Format Optimization

**STOP:**
- Generic checklist/bullet list infographics
- "5 tips for X" framing without edge
- Educational tone without exposé angle

**START:**
- **COMPARISON visuals:** "What they say" vs "What it means"
- **SINGLE STAT visuals:** One shocking number, large and bold
- **FLOW DIAGRAMS:** "Follow the money" - where dollars actually go
- **URGENCY timelines:** Red zones, closing windows

| Exposé Carousels | 30% | Save-worthy reference content |

#### Why This Worked

This post reached 41,594 impressions with NO visual content, proving that a strong hook on a trending topic can dramatically outperform carousels and infographics. The algorithm rewards engagement, and text-only posts with strong hooks generate immediate engagement.

**Tease Hook Formula:**
```
[Provocative setup that hints at revelation]

[Specific number/stat that means nothing without context]

...show more...

[Rest of post with explanation, context, and insights]
```

#### Apply To:
- Wednesday POV text posts
- Any post where the hook is stronger than any visual could be
- Trending/controversial topics where curiosity drives engagement
- Topics where Ginny's authority adds credibility to specific data points
