## Hard guardrails (every use)
1. **Headline the contracted number ($78.7M), not offered ($86.7M).** Offered = "identified savings opportunities" only.
2. **25% is the RFP rate, never "average."** Contracts 8.4%, PBRs 18.7%; a blend is lower.
3. **Use only these verified numbers.** No extrapolation, no invented per-post figures (no-fabricated-statistics rule).
4. **Vary the line.** Do not reuse the same anchor sentence within ~4 posts; rotate phrasing even when reusing a metric.
5. **Match the metric to the topic** (table below) so the anchor reads earned, not bolted on.
