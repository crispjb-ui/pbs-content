REBATES — SECTION 5.03
PBM shall pass through one hundred percent (100%) of all rebates received by PBM from manufacturers...
"Received," not "paid." A rebate aggregator the PBM owns takes its cut first. 100% of what is left is still 100%. The noun does the work.
